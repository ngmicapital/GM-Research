⚖️ Regulatory & Legal Radar

Paired with the SEC-CFTC MOU (March 11) establishing coordination protocols, the US regulatory regime has pivoted from hostile to "minimum effective dose." The market hasn't priced this structural shift — partly because sentiment is at extreme fear — but it changes the long-term risk/reward for US-listed crypto firms significantly.

  • Digital Asset Market Structure Bill — Still in Senate; SEC/CFTC joint framework provides interim clarity while bill advances. Committee markup expected Q2 2026.
  • SEC-CFTC MOU (signed March 11) — Formal coordination protocol; dual-regulator approach with "innovation-first" mandate. Foley & Lardner notes enforcement priorities now at historic lows.

  • SEC Crypto Assets Interpretation (March 17, 2026) — Token taxonomy issued; digital commodities, digital collectibles, stablecoins, digital securities defined. Non-securities: BTC, ETH, most PoW/PoS L1s.
